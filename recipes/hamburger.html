Delicious Hamburger Recipe

Description

This recipe will help you make your very own mindblowing hamburger for those special nights. While this recipe starts off simply by getting a nice sear on an 80/20 patty, it's what you do after that matters! Excellent spice selection and treatment of the hamburger bun will leave you and your guests wishing you had made a couple more.

Ingredients

  • One beef patty per person (80/20 preferred, but you can do you)
  • Hamburger Bun -- ideally with sesame seeds
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black Pepper (to taste)
  • Doogle Leaves (three full leaves)
  • Gnome spice (one shaker's worth)
  • Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat a pan up to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Prepare your patty by seasoning it with salt and pepper prior to putting it on the pan.
  3. Once the pan is preheated, put the patty on it.
  4. Flip it once every 1m30s. You will need four flips in total.
  5. On the final flip, put both halves of the bun face down on the pan for 30s each.
  6. Remove the patty and let it sit on a rack for 1m.
  7. Remove the bun and season it generously with everything bagel seasoning, your leaves, and your gnome spice.
  8. Place the patty on the bun and enjoy!
