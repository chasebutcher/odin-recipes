Crispy Chicken

Description

This recipe will help you make a chicken breast that is sure to knock your friends dead! It's so delicious and, thanks to our special cooking technique, doesn't lose any of its juice. Plus, this is one of our lowest effort recipes and is sure to be accessible to all!

Ingredients

  • A chicken breast
  • Water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put about a tbsp of salt into 1/4 gallon of water.
  2. Make sure the salt is dissolved so you have a briny mixture.
  3. Put the chicken breast in there for 30 minutes -- not too long!
  4. That's it. That's the recipe. Eat it.
