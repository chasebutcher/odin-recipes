French Fried Potatoes

Description

This simple recipe will guide you through the process of turning some classic Russet potatoes into an excellent side that makes you and your guests feel like you're at gourmet French restaurant (they serve fries, right?). You'll start by soaking the potatoes and then double-frying them for an unbeatable crunch!

Ingredients

  • Three russet potatoes (1/3lb each)
  • Cold water to cover the potatoes two or three times
  • Salt
  • Oil to shallow or deep fry

Steps

  1. Slice up all of the potatoes into desired fry shape. You can leave on or cut off the skin.
  2. Begin by soaking all of the potatoes in the cold water for 15 minutes.
  3. Rinse them off and soak them again in a new bowl of cold water.
  4. The water should be clear -- if it is not, rinse and soak again.
  5. When the starch is fully removed (what was clouding the water), gently remove the potatoes
  6. Preheat a pan or pot to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  7. Slowly add your cut up potatoes. Don't overcrowd the pan!
  8. When they're all finished, remove them and let them cool in a freezer for 15 minutes.
  9. Fry them again in the same oil. This is the last time!
  10. Remove them from the oil when golden brown and extremely crispy. Top with salt. Enjoy!
